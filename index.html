About Founder Karan Sir

The founder of Gainer’s Academy, Mr. Karan Gupta aims to implement the famous belief of Albert Einstein, “Never regard study as a duty but as an enviable opportunity to learn to know the liberating influence of beauty in the realm of the spirit for your own personal joy and to the profit of the community to which your later works belong” while blending the one-liner mission and vision of the Academy into the students’ life- Join- Learn- Achieve- Gain. In order to bear the fruits of his beliefs, Mr. Karan has enriched himself with knowledge in the field of Commerce, having degrees in CS, B.com, B.ed, LLB. He has acquired tremendous dexterity over the domain through a multitude of experiences involving 5 years of home-tutoring and running a coaching class since the last 5 years. He is also a Faculty at the esteemed Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. The roots of his knowledge are disseminated over the domains of Economics, Management and Entrepreneurship. He is an excellent mentor and guide a student can get who is striving to his/ her career in the right trajectory.
